!!!Add a button (Form control)

1. If the Developer tab is not available, display it.

2. On the Developer tab, in the Controls group, click Insert, and then under Form Controls, click Button [{Image src='Button.gif'}].

[{Image src='Controls.gif'}]

3. Click the worksheet location where you want the upper-left corner of the button to appear.

4. Assign a macro to the button.

5. To specify the control properties of the button, right-click the button, and then click Format Control.

----
!! Display the Developer tab

1. Click the __Microsoft Office Button__ , and then click __Excel Options__.

[{Image src='Microsoft.gif'}]

2. In the __Popular__ category, under __Top options for working with Excel__, select the __Show Developer tab in the Ribbon__ check box, and then click __OK__.
